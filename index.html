About Us

Risico Consulting is a 100% BEE owned company based in Johannesburg, South Africa.

Our focus lies in providing sustainable solutions to our clients in the area of Health, Safety, Quality and Environmental management.

Specialising in road transport, our management systems ensure compliance with legislation for the transportation of non-hazardous products through to dangerous goods.

Being an accredited Chemical & Allied Industries Association (CAIA) SQAS-AFRICA auditor and having completed ISO 9001 Lead Auditor certification, attention to detail is a core fundamental of our approach to management system design and implementation.

Ensuring logistics companies confidently align their quality management systems with SQAS-AFRICA requirements, legislation and customer requirements.
